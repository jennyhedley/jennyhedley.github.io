Jenny Hedley

Jenny Hedley is a third-year Creative Writing student at RMIT. In 2017 she completed a Diploma of Professional Writing and Editing, also at RMIT.

Jenny's writing appears or is forthcoming in Cordite, Verity La, SCUM, FOLIO, Travel Play Live, Gone Lawn, The Account and other publications. She recorded her poem 'I Can See Through Your Lululemons' for Memoria Podcast. Her work is complex and confronting, as she addresses mental illness, domestic violence, sexual assault, single parenting and other issues from a position of lived experience.

In between freelancing, studying, volunteering, and teaching her son how to hike, surf and SUP, she's drafting an experimental memoir that speaks to gendered violence and fractured memory. She lives on unceded Boon Wurrung land with her son.
